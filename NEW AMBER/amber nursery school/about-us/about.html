We are an Active Learning Preschool.

Preparing students academically, socially, and emotionally for elementary school.​

HISTORY

Amber Nursery School has been opened with the vision to provide not only a quality and value based education but to give every child an opportunity to grow up naturally and develop their, personality to face all the new challenges of the Global World.

Amber Nursery School is situated in B-67, Shastri Nagar,Meerut, Uttar Pradesh .

It is co educational senior secondary school with English as a medium of instruction.

Campus of Amber Nursery School is aesthetically designed and having lush green lawns and providing a child eco friendly environment. A well stocked liabrary, fully equipped and well designed labs(Science and language) and latest technology based computer labs adorns the school premises.The school has a consulting cell to resolve the various problems of students and parents both. The school not only lays emphasis on a wholesome education but is also providing a wide range of Co-curricullar activities such as MUSIC, DANCE, ART and CRAFT, DRAMATICS, SCULPTURE, GAMES, SCATING, SHOOTING AND YOGA with other important activities .Some selected celebrations such as teacher`s day, children`s day, Gandhi Jyanti, Independence day, Republic day etc.will be celebrated with full enthusiasm and zeal.

THE SCHOOL GIVE PRIME PRIORITY TO DICIPLINE AND MORALITY CHARACTER AND PATRIOTISM.

Last but not the least school embodies some of the good features of "Nursery school" focusing on "Indian Culture and values" without raising the of education.

MISSION

The relentless pursuit of the school has been to lay, The foundation of a Nobel character of Moral integrity, Brotherhood, Patriotism and Sacrifice, in the young minds, so that they can grow straight and strong, as Ideal Leader who can shape the destiny of the nations.

MOTTO

"उठो ! जागो ! और तब तक मत रुको जब तक अपना लक्ष्य न प्राप्त कर लो "

is the moto of the school. Which means "Arise awake don't stop till the goal is reached." So that every child has to work hard to be a good citizen and a good person of the society. To make this possible our school has brought all the modern facilities to educate them culturally, aesthetically and morally.

AIM

The aim of the school is to empower individuals with knowledge, skills, values and cultural identity. So that they may excel in their chosen fields. Self discipline and sense of responsibility are the qualities we foster, to provide a firm foundation for the years ahead. We seek to encourge the awareness of the need for courtesy and consideration for others.
