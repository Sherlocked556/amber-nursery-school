HEALTHCARE

All students are subjected to through medical checkup once in a month and the reports will be sent to the parents for their information for taking remedial measure where necessary. The M.I. room of Amber Nursery School caters to first aid treatment for all ailments and injuries to the students of this school. Trained nurse has been appointed for the purpose.
